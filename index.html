About Agro-Reap Organics

Repurposing Food Waste

At Agro-Reap Organics, we're committed to tackling climate change, fostering green livelihoods, and building sustainable food systems. We believe that food waste isn't waste at all, but a valuable resource waiting to be harnessed. Through transformative solutions, we empower communities and pave the way for a healthier planet.

Our role

What we do

Agro-Reap Organics addresses climate change and fosters green livelihoods by implementing sustainable practices. In the realm of climate change mitigation, the organization focuses on reducing greenhouse gas emissions by diverting organic waste from landfills, producing biogas as a clean energy source, and enhancing soil health through organic fertilizers. In terms of green livelihoods, Agro-Reap Organics creates jobs in waste collection and fertilizer production, empowers farmers with organic fertilizers for improved crop yields, and promotes sustainable practices through community education. The organization also contributes to building sustainable food systems by diverting food waste, transforming waste into organic fertilizers to close the nutrient loop, and enhancing food security through healthier soils and increased crop yields for communities.
